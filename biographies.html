Famous For : Saha Ionization Equation

Meghnad Saha was a pioneering astrophysicist whose work fundamentally changed our understanding of stars. Born in a modest family in Bengal, Saha rose to international prominence through his groundbreaking work in thermal ionization . His most notable contribution, the Saha Ionization Equation , explained how the physical and chemical conditions in stars affect their light spectra. Saha was not only a scientist but also a science policy maker. He was instrumental in establishing several scientific institutions in India, including the Institute of Nuclear Physics in Kolkata. As a member of parliament, he advocated for using science to solve national problems like power generation and infrastructure.
